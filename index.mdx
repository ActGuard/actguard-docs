Add **budgets**, **execution controls**, and **workflow verification** — without changing how your models are called.

## What you can do

**Set clear limits.**  
Control token usage, USD spend, and model consumption per agent or tenant.

**Let agents use real tools.**  
Connect to Google Workspace, Notion, Stripe, and internal APIs — more integrations coming soon.

**Run tools safely.**  
Apply rate limits, retries, timeouts, and idempotency automatically.

**Require verification before action.**  
Ensure identifiers were fetched and required steps occurred before side effects.

**Stay observable.**  
Emit runtime events for cost, tool usage, and enforcement decisions.
